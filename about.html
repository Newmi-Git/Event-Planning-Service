Who We Are

About Us

Affair De Coeur is an event planning company dedicated to creating memorable, stress-free, and meaningful celebrations. We understand that every event is unique, which is why we work closely with our clients to transform their ideas into unforgettable experiences. Whether it is a wedding, birthday celebration, corporate function, graduation, or special occasion, our team is committed to handling every detail with professionalism and creativity.

Through our event planning services, budgeting tools, venue recommendations, and personalised support, we help clients stay organised while bringing their vision to life. Our goal is to simplify the planning process and ensure that every event reflects the personality, style, and expectations of our clients. At Affair De Coeur, we believe that successful events are built on careful planning, strong communication, and attention to detail.

Why We Do This

We are passionate about helping people celebrate life's most important moments. We know that planning an event can be overwhelming and time-consuming, which is why we created a platform that provides everything clients need in one convenient place. Our mission is to make event planning simple, organised, and unforgettable while reducing the stress that often comes with coordinating special occasions.

We do what we do because we believe every event tells a story. Whether it is bringing families together, recognising achievements, or creating opportunities for people to connect, events have the power to create lasting memories. By combining creativity, organisation, and innovation, we strive to turn ideas into extraordinary experiences that inspire, celebrate, and leave a positive impact on everyone involved.
