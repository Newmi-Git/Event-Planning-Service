About Us

Affair De Coeur is an event planning company dedicated to creating memorable and stress-free celebrations. We help clients organise weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and special occasions through professional planning, budgeting tools, venue recommendations, and personalised support.

Our mission is to make event planning simple , organised and unforgettable by providing everything our clients need in one place. We want to transform ideas into extraordinary events that inspire , celebrate and leave lasting memories.
